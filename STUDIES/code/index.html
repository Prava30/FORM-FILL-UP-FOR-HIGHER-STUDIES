Higher Studies



What is Education ?



Education is a purposeful activity directed at achieving certain aims, such as transmitting knowledge or fostering skills and character traits. These aims may include the development of understanding, rationality, kindness, and honesty.



As per experts, education refers to a gradual process that brings about positive changes in human life and behavior, thereby developing their personalities.The education system refers to the entire process that a student has to undergo in order to acquire knowledge.This is implemented by way of instructions or some other practical procedures.



So, Education is the basic right of every individual all around the world because this is a trait that encourages them to distinguish between right and wrong and helps them to achieve their goal.



Types of Education :




Education refers to the development of the learning and thinking process. It goes beyond the four walls of the classroom. It is all about gaining experience.


We can divide education into three main types:


  • 1 Formal Education: This is also known as formal learning which usually takes place within the premises of the school. It refers to the basic academic knowledge that a child learns in a formal manner.This continues from an elementary school to secondary school and further on to colleges. Such an education is provided by specially qualified teachers who are efficient enough with the art of instruction.
  • 2 Informal Education: This is the type of education wherein a parent is teaching a child things that are beyond academics like preparing a meal or riding a bicycle.People can also get informal education through books or educational websites. This is an education that is not derived in schools through a proper learning method. It is not pre-planned nor deliberate.
  • 3 Non-formal Education: It refers to adult basic education, adult literacy education, or skill development. It can take different forms of learning, which is consistently and systematically provided in order to develop a particular skill or ability in an individual.



Why is higher studies so important?




The benefits of education are many. Not only will you personally benefit from receiving education when it comes to income, career advancement, skill development, and employment opportunities, but your society and community receive benefits of education as well.Societies with higher rates of degree completion and levels of education tend to be healthier, have higher rates of economic stability, lower crime, and greater equality.




Benefits of Education are socual and personal; Some of them are following below-

  • 1. Healthier Lifestyle
  • 2. Pursuing Your Passion
  • 3. Personal Development of Skills
  • 4. Experimentation and Diversity are a Benefit of Education
  • 5. Socializing and Networking are Personal Benefits of Education
  • 6. More Employment is a Benefit of Education
  • 7. Career Entry and Advancement
  • 8. Economic Growth
  • 9. Environmental Benefits
  • 10. Societal Benefits
